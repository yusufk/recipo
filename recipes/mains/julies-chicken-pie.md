# Julie's Chicken Pie

## Ingredients

- Boneless chicken breast or thighs, cut into pieces
- Lemon juice
- Salt & pepper
- Mustard powder
- 1 large potato
- 1 tablespoon chow-chow or piccalilli
- Tomato sauce
- Mustard sauce
- Boiled eggs, sliced (optional)
- Grated cheese
- Dough (from Indian Delights — Mince in scones recipe)
- 1 beaten egg (for glaze)
- Poppy seeds

## Method

1. Cook the chicken with lemon juice, salt, pepper, and mustard until thoroughly cooked and tender.
2. Break the cooked chicken into small shreds or mince finely.
3. Boil one large potato until tender. Mash until smooth — no lumps.
4. In a bowl, mix the shredded chicken with the mashed potato.
5. Add approximately 1 tablespoon of chow-chow or piccalilli. Stir to combine.
6. Roll out half the dough to fit the bottom of a square baking tin. Line the tin.
7. Spread a layer of tomato sauce and mustard sauce over the dough base.
8. Distribute the chicken and potato filling evenly over the sauced base.
9. Add sliced boiled eggs over the filling (if using).
10. Sprinkle a generous layer of grated cheese over everything.
11. Roll out the remaining dough and place over the filling as a top crust.
12. Make small holes in the top with a fork to allow steam to escape.
13. Brush with beaten egg and sprinkle with poppy seeds.
14. Bake at 180°C for 30-45 minutes until the crust is golden brown.
15. Allow to cool slightly before slicing and serving.

## Notes

- A Julie (mom's) recipe — the one everyone asks for
- The chow-chow/piccalilli is the secret ingredient — gives it a distinctive tangy kick
- Use the dough recipe from "Indian Delights — Mince in scones" for the crust
- The egg layer is optional but recommended
- Goes beautifully with a fresh garden salad
