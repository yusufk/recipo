# Chicken Pie

## Ingredients

- Chicken meat (thighs or breast, cooked)
- 1 large potato
- Lemon juice
- Salt & pepper
- Mustard (dry or prepared)
- 1 tablespoon chow-chow or piccalilli
- Tomato sauce
- Mustard sauce
- Boiled eggs (optional)
- Grated cheese
- Dough (from Indian Delights — Mince in scones recipe)
- Beaten egg (for glazing)
- Poppy seeds

## Method

1. Cook meat in lemon, salt, pepper and mustard until tender
2. Break into small shreds
3. Make mash of 1 big potato
4. Mix meat & mash, add about 1 tablespoon chow-chow or piccalilli
5. Add tomato sauce and mustard sauce to taste
6. Layer in pie dish: meat mixture, sliced boiled eggs, grated cheese
7. Cover with dough (use Indian Delights "Mince in scones" recipe for the dough)
8. Glaze with beaten egg and sprinkle with poppy seeds
9. Bake at 180°C until golden brown (approximately 35-45 minutes)

## Notes

- Use dough recipe from "Indian Delights - Mince in scones"
- The chow-chow gives it a distinctive tangy kick
- Goes well with a fresh salad on the side
- Family recipe — passed down through generations
