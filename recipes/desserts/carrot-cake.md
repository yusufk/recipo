# Carrot Cake

## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 1 cup sugar
- 1.5 cups grated peeled carrots
- 1 cup chopped pecan nuts
- 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
- 1 cup vegetable oil
- 1.5 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon bicarbonate of soda
- 4 eggs

### Frosting

- 1 x 250g tub cream cheese
- 1 tablespoon soft butter
- 1 teaspoon vanilla essence
- 3 cups icing sugar

## Method

1. Beat sugar with eggs until light and fluffy
2. Add oil, carrots, nuts and remaining dry ingredients
3. Beat together until well combined
4. Pour into a greased baking tin
5. Bake at 180°C for 30 minutes (or 20 minutes for cupcakes)
6. Allow to cool completely before frosting

### Frosting

1. Beat cream cheese and butter together until smooth
2. Add vanilla essence
3. Gradually add icing sugar, beating until smooth and spreadable
4. Spread over cooled cake

## Notes

- Works beautifully as cupcakes — reduce baking time to 20 minutes
- The frosting is generous — halve it if you prefer a lighter coating
- Keeps well in the fridge for 3-4 days
